Pixelit's Frequently Asked Questions

Created on 4/4/2024

What is Pixelit?

Pixelit is an upcoming Blooket private server focused on creating a pixel-themed blooket private server. This project is made by IzumiiHD, SOUNDGOD, and Iamgamedude written in NodeJS.

When will Pixelit be released?

Pixelit has a scheduled release date for June 2024. We will keep you guys updated every week on our discord.

How old do I have to be in order to play?

To play Pixelit you have to be 13 years and older. We do allow underaged users if they are mature enough to play.

Getting staff is not easy, you will have to be really active in our community, and you will have to be a good person. To get staff you will first need verified but we could make exceptions.

What do I do in Pixelit?

Pixelit is a place where you can play with friends, open wraps to collect pixils, chat with people, and more!
